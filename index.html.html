About the Club

Badminton for Every Level

Q Badminton Club — the Q stands for Kew, the area of Southport where the club has its roots — is a non-league social club that has been welcoming adult players since the 1990s. We're not about league tables or pressure — just great badminton, good company, and a warm welcome to anyone who wants to pick up a racket.

Whether you're from Kew, Birkdale, Ainsdale or anywhere across Southport and the wider Merseyside area, you'll find a friendly, inclusive club here. We play mixed, men's and ladies' doubles throughout the evening, so you'll get plenty of court time whatever your preferred style.

Common Questions

FAQs

What does the Q stand for in Q Badminton Club?
The Q stands for Kew — the area of Southport where the club originated. The full name is Kew Badminton Club, though we're known locally as Q Badminton.
When does Q Badminton Club meet in Southport?
We meet every Thursday evening from 8pm to 10pm at Dunes Leisure Centre.
Where exactly is the club based?
The club has its roots in the Kew area of Southport. We now play at Dunes Leisure Centre on Marine Drive, Southport, Merseyside — open to players from across the town and beyond.
Is this club suitable for beginners or rusty players?
Absolutely. We're a non-league social club, so all abilities are welcome. Whether you played regularly years ago or are completely new to badminton, you'll find a friendly atmosphere here.
What type of badminton do you play?
We play doubles throughout the evening — mixed doubles, men's doubles, and ladies' doubles, depending on who's in attendance on the night.
What does it cost to play?
Your first evening is free. After that, there is a modest session fee or membership arrangement — contact the club for current details.
Do I need to book in advance?
No advance booking is needed. Simply come along on any Thursday evening and introduce yourself.
